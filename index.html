CARE4 is a product created to serve victims of Alzheimer’s disease. More than a simple geolocation device, it is meant to show the patient that somebody cares deeply about him/her and that there’s always somebody he/she can get in touch with.

Our product will be developed thinking not only in the patient but also about its relatives who invest all of their time, energy, and love taking care of him/her. CARE4 is meant to alleviate somewhat the constant worry about the patient’s condition. It does not replace physical presence; however, it allows the patient not to feel that all his/her independence and privacy are lost due to the illness.

CARE4
- tablet app -

  • Quick preview of vital signs with additional features to control mental health.

  • Control patient's medical history on the tablet for easy access.

  • Real time information about patient's location plus potential danger alerts.

  • Keep track of events and appointments and get alerts when they are about to happen.

  • An easy way to control medication intake and track its effects.

  • Send quick messages to the patient or relevant contacts.

  • Quick access to relevant emergency contacts.

  • The settings tab allows the user to customize functionality and information that will be populated both in the tablet and the watch.

CARE4
- watch -

  • The caregiver may introduce facts about the patient's life for moments when he/she doesn't remember.

  • The patient has the possibility of sending voice messages tho his/her loved ones or caregiver.

  • For the patient to feel constantly connected to his/her family, friends, and doctors, pictures can be loaded into the watch.

  • Offers real time location. If he/she is lost, there is a 'Take me Home' option which will also send an alert to the caregiver.

  • A bot can be specifically tailored to the life and needs of the patient.

  • Music has been proved to have a positive effect on people's memory. The patient can have access to a number of his/her favourite songs.

  • Images and video to boost memories.

*Also available for color-blind people
